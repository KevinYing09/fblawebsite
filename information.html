Policies

  • Clean up all trash

    Please keep the cleanliness of our building in mind when attending events. This saves our faculty lots of time and allows us to keep hosting public events.

  • No smoking

    Skyline enforces a strict no smoking policy in order to protect the health of our faculty, students, and guests. Violators will be removed from the property.

  • Be respectful

    All guests must be respectful to performers, falculty, and other guests in order to uphold the reputation of Skyline and its students.

  • No throwing objects

    In order to protect the safety of performers, faculty, and guests please refrain from throwing objects. Violators will be removed from the property.

Amenities

  • Concessions

    Concessions are available for purchase at the concession stand located to the right of the entrance. Outside food and drink are prohibited.

  • Restrooms

    Restrooms are located on the left side of the entrance. Please keep them clean and report any issues to a faculty member.

  • First Aid

    First aid kits are provided in case of an emergency. Please contact a faculty member if you are in need of aid or other assistance.

  • Lost and Found

    A lost and found is located in the coach's office on the north end of the lower floor. Ask a faculty member to accompany you as the doors may be locked.
